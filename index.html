What is this?

The Immersive Design System is a set of guidelines to advance traditional design systems allowing creators to become fully immersed and, ultimately, deliver a better product.

It helps us look beyond what is right in front of us and consider more deeply where and who we are shipping to.

The World

The World describes the living environment our users and creators are working within and which our product must be delivered in.

Consider the unique challenges, opportunities and requirements within any given world to optimise your product.

Users

Our Users describe the individuals who each have specific expectations and responsibilities within their respective world.

Consider the unique challenges, opportunities and requirements for any given user persona to optimise your product.

Product

The Product is what creators ultimately put out to cater for user needs within the world. Creators should be able to explore and discover the product and it's foundations without boundaries.

Each section of a product should tie into your user personas and adhere to constraints, while optimising for requirements, of the world.

Creators

The Creators are the individuals who are developing the Product. They are designers, engineers, product managers, etc.

Immerse your creators in the relevant world and expose them to users to broaden the opportunity of what can be produced.
